## Module 3: Love, Intimacy & Communication (Age 25)

**Q7:** Your partner wants to discuss moving in together, but you're not ready. How do you respond?
- "Let's talk about it later" (Avoid)
- "Why are you pressuring me?" (Defensive)
- "I hear this is important to you. Can we talk about both our concerns?" (Active listening)
- "I need more time. Let's revisit this in 3 months?" (Boundary + timeline)

**Q8:** During a disagreement, your partner raises their voice. You:
- Raise your voice back to match their energy
- Shut down completely and stop talking
- "I need us both to lower our voices so we can hear each other"
- "Let's take a 20-minute break and come back to this"

**Q9:** Your partner forgets an important date. Which statement is most effective?
- "You never remember anything important to me!" (Attack)
- Don't say anything, just act cold and distant (Passive-aggressive)
- "I feel hurt when dates I care about are forgotten. Can we find a system?" (I-statement)
- "It's fine" (but you're still upset) (Dismissive)

**Q10:** Who does the emotional labor in your relationship?

- Mostly your partner
- Shared fairly equally - we both contribute
- Not sure what "emotional labor" means

**Q11:** Relationship status decision:

## Scoring Systems

### Module 3: Communication Score
- **Range:** 0-8 points
- **Based on:** Healthy communication patterns (active listening, I-statements, de-escalation, shared emotional labor)

### Module 4: Compatibility Score
- **Range:** 0-10 points
- **Based on:** Homogamy (education, values, finances, children agreement)

### Module 7: Resilience Score
- **Range:** 1-8+ points
- **Based on:** Number of support systems + relationship strengthening
