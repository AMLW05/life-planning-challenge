## üéÆ About This Game

The **Life Planning Challenge** is an interactive decision-making game threaded throughout SOC-213 (Sociology of the Family). Students make sequential life decisions across 8 modules and experience how choices in one domain (education, career, relationships, family formation, finances) cascade through all others.

**This game is NOT a test.** There are no "right" or "wrong" decisions. The goal is to demonstrate how family is an interconnected system shaped by structural forces, not just personal choices.

---

## üéØ Learning Objectives

Through this game, students will:

1. **Experience Systems Thinking:** Understand how family decisions are interconnected, not isolated
2. **Recognize Structural Constraints:** See how economics, gender, education, and policy constrain "personal" choices
3. **Apply Life Course Perspective:** Experience how early decisions affect later options
4. **Build Empathy:** Try different paths and understand diverse family experiences
5. **Connect Theory to Experience:** Reflect on game experiences using sociological concepts and research

## üìã Game Structure

### 8 Modules Corresponding to Course Content

| Module | Title | Time | Description |
|--------|-------|------|-------------|
| **1** | Character Creation & Values | 15 min | Reflect on family background and personal values |
| **2** | Education & Career Path | 20 min | Choose education level and career field |
| **3** | Love, Intimacy & Communication | 20 min | Navigate relationship scenarios and communication strategies |
| **4** | Sexuality & Mate Selection | 20 min | Assess compatibility and experience homogamy patterns |
| **5** | Marriage & Partnership Formation | 20 min | Decide whether to marry, cohabit, or maintain independence |
| **6** | Parenthood & Work-Family Balance | 25 min | Make childcare decisions and manage time/money budgets |
| **7** | Family Challenges & Transitions | 20 min | Navigate crises (job loss, financial stress, family conflicts) |
| **8** | Systems Reflection & Analysis | 25 min | Map decision cascades and reflect on entire journey |

**Total Play Time:** ~3 hours across 8 modules (played at your own pace throughout the semester)

## üé® Design Philosophy

### **Pedagogical Principles:**

1. **Experiential Learning:** Students learn by doing, not just reading
2. **Consequential Choices:** Decisions have visible, realistic consequences
3. **No Judgment:** All paths are valid; goal is understanding, not "winning"
4. **Connected to Theory:** Every game element ties to sociological concepts
5. **Reflection-Driven:** Game experience becomes data for sociological analysis

### **Game Design Principles:**

1. **Realistic Scenarios:** Based on sociological research and real family experiences
2. **Structural Forces Visible:** Economics, gender, time, resources shown explicitly
3. **Cascading Consequences:** Early decisions constrain/enable later options
4. **Multiple Paths:** Students can replay to see different outcomes
5. **Data Exportable:** Students own and control their game data

## üìö Sociological Concepts Demonstrated

The game makes these abstract sociological concepts concrete and experiential:

- **Systems Thinking:** Family as interconnected system
- **Life Course Perspective:** Timing and sequencing of life events
- **Structural Constraints:** How institutions shape choices
- **Intersectionality:** How gender, class, race affect opportunities
- **Work-Family Conflict:** Time squeeze and resource tradeoffs
- **Economic Inequality:** How resources shape family formation
- **Homogamy:** Tendency to partner within same education/class
- **Second Shift:** Gendered household labor
- **Family Diversity:** Multiple valid family forms

## üêõ Troubleshooting

### **My progress disappeared!**
- Did you clear your browser data? That deletes localStorage.
- Did you switch browsers? Each browser has separate storage.
- **Solution:** Export your data regularly! Upload exports to Canvas.

### **The export button isn't working!**
- Make sure you've completed at least one module first.
- Check if downloads are blocked in your browser.
- Try a different browser.

### **I want to restart the game completely.**
- Open browser console (F12)
- Type: `localStorage.removeItem('lifePlanningGame')`
- Refresh the page

### **Mobile version looks weird.**
- The game is mobile-responsive, but best experienced on desktop/tablet
- Try rotating your device to landscape mode
